Session Preparation Guide

  1. Ensure you have the correct date, time and duration of the reading.
  2. Create a calm space, preferably where you are not disturbed by noise or any other intervention during the reading.
  3. Keep an open mind and set clear intentions about what you want to achieve or gain from the experience. This decides how successful the reading will be.
  4. Be mindful of the time the reading is booked for. Sometimes an extension may not be possible.
  5. Have some questions written down before your session. Being specific helps.
  6. Write down all the messages that are communicated during the reading.
  7. Clarify and confirm if you have any doubts.

Often additional information (things you've not asked guidance for) is shared. Identify and book another session to address them, if required.
